Eight Sleep Smart Mattress

Individualized Climate Controls

Advanced mattress technology with individualized climate controls that dynamically adjusts temperature throughout the night for optimal sleep cycles.

Setup & Controls

  1. Open the Eight Sleep app on the provided iPhone
  2. Use the general profile, add your basic information (age, gender, typical bedtime etc.) and set preferred sleep temperature
  3. Select your side of the bed within the app
  4. Set or confirm your wake-up time for gentle temperature-based awakening
  5. Allow automatic adjustments throughout sleep cycles
  6. Do not log out from the app – the system will be reset for each guest
